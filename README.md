## 🎭 About the Game

**FREQUENCY 333** is a 2–3 level psychological thriller text-adventure built around the Echo Chain true-crime podcast universe.

### The Story
Your team arrives at an abandoned radio station — WKTZ, shut down in 1974 after an unexplained incident. Every person known to have heard a complete broadcast of **Frequency 333** has vanished. Tonight, the signal is live again. The door locks behind you.

### Structure
| Level | Location | Puzzles |
|-------|----------|---------|
| 1 | The Station — Control Room | Dial Cipher + Tape Reel Order |
| 2 | The Sound Room + Archives | Frequency Grid + Listener's Ledger + Mandela Puzzle |
| 3 | The Broadcast Booth | Final Frequency Puzzle |
| Ending | The Silent Vote | 4 endings |

### Puzzles
- **📡 Date Shifting Cipher** — Tune the radio dial to a historical timestamp (11/03/65)
- **🎞 Tape Loop Puzzle** — Order 4 tape reels: Storm → Silence → Watcher → Name
- **🔊 Frequency Grid** — Activate a cross pattern without triggering danger nodes
- **📒 Listener's Ledger** — Cross-reference disappearance dates to find the first victim year
- **🧠 Mandela Puzzle** — Find the one constant word across 4 conflicting memories
- **📻 Final Frequency** — Name what the broadcast feeds on (CURIOSITY)
